# Refund Policy for WontLetYou

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 18, 2026

## 7-Day Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with WontLetYou. If you're not happy with your purchase, we offer a **7-day no-questions-asked refund policy**.

## How to Request a Refund

Since all payments are processed through Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android), refunds must be requested through these platforms:

### iOS Users (Apple App Store)

1. Visit Apple's Report a Problem page: https://reportaproblem.apple.com
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find your WontLetYou purchase
4. Select "Request a Refund"
5. Choose your reason and submit

Alternatively, contact Apple Support: https://support.apple.com/billing

### Android Users (Google Play Store)

1. Go to https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637
2. Follow Google's refund request instructions
3. Or open Google Play Store → Menu → Account → Purchase History → Find WontLetYou → Request a Refund

## Refund Eligibility

- **Within 7 days:** Full refund, no questions asked
- **After 7 days:** Refunds are subject to Apple's and Google's standard refund policies (typically only granted in exceptional circumstances)

## What Happens After a Refund

- Your subscription will be canceled immediately
- You will lose access to premium features
- Your data will remain in the App (unless you delete your account separately)

## Cancellations Without Refunds

If you simply want to cancel your subscription (without a refund), you can do so at any time:

- **iOS:** Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → WontLetYou → Cancel Subscription
- **Android:** Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → WontLetYou → Cancel Subscription

**Canceling stops future charges** but does not refund the current billing period. You retain access until the end of the paid period.

## Important Notes

- **We do not process refunds directly.** All refunds are handled by Apple and Google according to their policies
- **Refund processing time** varies by platform (typically 3-5 business days for Apple, 1-4 business days for Google)
- **One-time purchases** (if applicable) follow the same 7-day refund policy
- **Free trials:** If you cancel during a free trial, you will not be charged

## Exceptions

In rare cases, we may offer refunds outside the 7-day window at our discretion (e.g., technical issues preventing App use). Contact us at wontletyousupport@gmail.com to discuss.

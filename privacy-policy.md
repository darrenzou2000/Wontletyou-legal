## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information (Optional):** If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and name
- **User Content:** All goals, tasks, photos, notes, and other data you input into the App
- **Payment Information:** Processed through Apple App Store and Google Play Store (we do not store payment details)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, timezone, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the App, features used, session duration
- **Analytics:** Built-in analytics to improve App performance and user experience

### Children Under 13

The App is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe we may have collected information from a child under 13 should contact us at wontletyousupport@gmail.com.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Process your subscription and manage your account (if created)
- Suggest goals and verify photos using AI services (see Third-Party Services below)
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Communicate with you about updates, support, and service-related notices
- Detect and prevent fraud or technical issues

## Third-Party Services

We share limited data with third-party services to provide specific features:

### AI Services (OpenAI and Anthropic)

When you use goal suggestions or photo verification features, we send relevant data (your input text, photos, and context) to OpenAI and/or Anthropic's AI services. These services process the data to generate suggestions or verify photos. We use Anthropic as a backup service for reliability.

**What this means:** Your goals, photos, and related inputs may be processed by these AI providers. We recommend not including sensitive personal information in goals or photos.

**Their policies:**
- OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
- Anthropic Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

### Payment Processors

All payments are processed through Apple In-App Purchase (iOS) and Google Play Billing (Android). We do not have access to your payment card details. Refer to Apple's and Google's privacy policies for how they handle payment information.

### Analytics

We use built-in analytics to track how users interact with the App. This helps us identify bugs, understand which features are popular, and improve the overall experience.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- **Deleted Accounts:** If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
- **Anonymous Analytics:** We may retain anonymized usage data indefinitely for analytics

# Delete Account – WontLetYou

If you'd like to delete your account, please do so directly within the app by following the steps below.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the **WontLetYou** app and go to **Home**
2. Tap the **Menu** icon at the top of the screen
3. Select the **Support** tab
4. Tap **Delete Account**

## Grace Period

To prevent accidental deletions, there is a **7-day grace period** after submitting your request. Your account will be permanently deleted after this period has passed.
